Disease Information

Recommendations

Organic Treatment

  • Use honey and ginger mixture to soothe throat
  • Try steam inhalation with eucalyptus oil
  • Drink turmeric milk to reduce inflammation

Chemical Treatment

  • Inhaled corticosteroids (e.g., fluticasone)
  • Short-acting beta agonists (e.g., albuterol)
  • Leukotriene modifiers (e.g., montelukast)

Environmental Treatment

  • Use air purifiers to reduce allergens
  • Keep living spaces clean and dust-free
  • Avoid exposure to smoke and strong fumes
